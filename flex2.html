BLUE:

blue is a primary color across all models of color space. It is the color of the ocean and the sky; it often symbolizes serenity, stability, inspiration, or wisdom.

Purple:

Purple is the most powerful visible wavelength out of all of the colors in the rainbow. Purple can symbolize many things, but to sum it up in a few words, purple represents strength, transformation, power, and royalty.

BLACK:

black, in physics, what is perceived with the human eye when light is absent or when all wavelengths in the visible spectrum are absorbed.
